# Micron Technology

Micron is the #3 HBM supplier and a major DRAM/NAND manufacturer.

## HBM Position

- **HBM market share:** 21% (Q3 2025, per Astute Group)
- "Our HBM capacity for calendar 2025 and 2026 is fully booked." — Sanjay Mehrotra, Micron CEO
- Requalification as an alternative HBM source is a strategic consideration for procurement leaders

## Market Position

- Major DRAM and NAND manufacturer
- Positioned as a potential alternative supplier for HBM3E as customers seek to diversify away from SK Hynix/Samsung duopoly
- HBM represents a growing revenue stream in the memory division

## Relationships

- Competing with [[sk-hynix]] and [[samsung]] for HBM market share
- Memory products supply AI GPU makers
