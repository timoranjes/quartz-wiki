# Lumentum

Lumentum is a leading manufacturer of electro-absorption modulated lasers (EMLs) and optical components for data center and telecom applications.

## Market Position

- One of fewer than 5 companies manufacturing EMLs at commercial scale (alongside Coherent, Mitsubishi, Sumitomo, Broadcom)
- **The only supplier shipping 200G-per-lane EMLs at volume** — required for next-generation 1.6T transceivers
- Investing in expanding its San Jose InP (indium phosphide) fabrication facility
- Analysts expect double-digit price increases on 200G EMLs in 2026 given absence of viable second-source suppliers

## NVIDIA Supply Agreement

- Received $2 billion commitment from NVIDIA (March 2, 2026) for EML laser supply
- Part of NVIDIA's $4 billion combined lockup with [[coherent]]
- Lead times for non-NVIDIA buyers stretched beyond 2027

## Technology

- EML technology: combines continuous light source and high-speed modulator on single chip
- Encodes data onto light signals at up to 200 gigabits per lane
- Indium phosphide fabrication yields range from 15% to 50% depending on wafer generation

## Relationships

- Primary EML supplier for [[nvidia]]
- Competes with [[coherent]], Mitsubishi, Sumitomo, Broadcom in EML market
- Supplies optical module assemblers including [[innolight]] and others
