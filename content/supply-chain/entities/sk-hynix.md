# SK Hynix

SK Hynix is the dominant leader in the HBM market and a key supplier of advanced memory for AI infrastructure.

## Market Position

- **HBM market share:** 57% (Q3 2025, per Astute Group)
- Set to supply ~70% of HBM4 units for NVIDIA's next-generation Rubin platform
- Surpassed Samsung in annual profits for the first time in 2025 — a symbolic event showcasing the tectonic shift HBM has brought to the industry

## HBM Technology

- Began mass production of HBM4 in February 2026, 3–4 months ahead of schedule
- HBM4 operating speed: 11.7Gbps (46% above JEDEC 8Gbps standard)
- Adopted femtosecond laser technology for wafer thinning (20–30 micrometers)
- Unveiled "H3" hybrid architecture integrating HBM + HBF

## HBF Strategy

- Co-development with SanDisk (NAND expertise)
- Targeting sample shipments H2 2026, mass production early 2027
- Pursuing industry standardization approach (vs. Samsung's solo development)

## Market Outlook

- 2026 HBM market projected at $54.6 billion, growing 58% YoY
- Analysts project SK Hynix targeting quarterly operating profit of KRW 30 trillion
- DRAM market expected to remain in shortage throughout 2026

## Relationships

- Primary HBM supplier for [[nvidia]] AI GPUs
- Competing with [[samsung]] and [[micron]] for HBM market share
- Partnering with SanDisk for HBF co-development
