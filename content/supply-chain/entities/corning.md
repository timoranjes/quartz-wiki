# Corning

Corning is a leading manufacturer of optical fiber, glass, and ceramics, and a critical supplier in the AI data center fiber optic supply chain.

## Market Position

- Leading optical fiber manufacturer
- Signed $6 billion supply agreement with Meta (January 2026)
- Two additional deals of similar scale with unnamed hyperscalers (Q1 2026 earnings)
- Received $300 million investment from NVIDIA to fund three new fiber manufacturing plants in North Carolina and Texas

## Technology

- Manufacturing optical fiber preforms — the glass rods from which fiber is drawn
- Preform expansion takes 18–24 months, limiting near-term supply regardless of downstream investment

## Market Dynamics

- Data center fiber demand grew ~76% YoY in 2025
- North American demand growth projected at 22–25% in 2026, supply expansion trails at 12–19%
- AI data centers need ~36x more fiber than traditional CPU server racks

## Relationships

- Supplying [[nvidia]] (investment + fiber)
- Supplying Meta, Google, Microsoft, and other hyperscalers
- Competing with Fujikura (Japan) in optical fiber market
